Features

Everything you need
to work with PDFs

Professional annotation tools built for speed and simplicity, with no account required.

✏️

Freehand Drawing

Draw directly on PDFs with smooth, pressure-sensitive ink strokes. Choose from multiple colors and brush sizes.

🖊️

Text Highlighting

Select and highlight any text with a single drag. Highlights are rendered as standard PDF annotations — compatible with all readers.

✍️

Signature & Stamps

Draw your signature once and place it anywhere on the document. Drag to reposition before confirming.

T

Add Text

Insert text annotations anywhere on the page. Full CJK support — type in Chinese, Japanese, or Korean with no issues.

↩️

Undo & Redo

Full undo/redo history for all annotation types. Powered by Chromium's ink stroke architecture for reliability.

🔍

Text Search

Search for any word or phrase across the current page. Results are highlighted instantly as you type.

💾

Save Anywhere

Save annotated PDFs directly back to your chosen location. No extra copies, no cluttered Downloads folder.

🔒

100% Private

All processing happens on your device. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server.
