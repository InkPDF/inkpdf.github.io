Privacy Policy for InkPDF

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how InkPDF ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application InkPDF (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • PDF Files: The App accesses PDF files only when you explicitly open them. File contents are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Annotations: Any annotations, signatures, or text you add to PDF files are stored locally on your device.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use the following third-party services that may collect data automatically:

Google Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. Firebase may collect:

  • App usage data and events
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country/region)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Firebase Analytics data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (can be reset in your device settings)
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data for ad personalization

For more information, see Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings under Google Settings → Ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the App's functionality
  • To analyze app usage and fix bugs (via Firebase)
  • To display relevant advertisements (via AdMob)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Google LLC – for Firebase Analytics and AdMob services

4. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Read: To open PDF files you select
  • Storage Write: To save annotated PDF files to your chosen location
  • Internet: Required for Firebase Analytics and AdMob to function

5. Data Retention

Recently opened file records are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App. Firebase and AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Reset your Advertising ID in your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Settings
  • Disable Firebase data collection by uninstalling the App

8. Security

All PDF file processing is performed locally on your device. We implement reasonable measures to protect information transmitted to third-party services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App's update release notes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Google Play developer page.
